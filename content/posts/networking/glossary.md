## CIDR

Classless Inter-Domain Routing, a method to allocate IP addresses

## Unicast

one receiver (HTTP request, SSH session, DNS query)

## Broadcast

everyone on the network (ARP ("Who has 192.168.1.1?"), DHCP discovery)

## Multicast

a subscribed group (Video streaming (IPTV), OSPF/BGP routing updates)

## Anycast

the nearest of many (Cloudflare/AWS DNS (1.1.1.1, 8.8.8.8), CDN edge selection, DDoS-resilient services)
