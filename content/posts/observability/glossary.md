## Dimension

key/value pair (region: "US-East", user_type: "premium", ...) of a metric (requests_per_second)

## Cardinality

total number of **unique** dimension combinations. For example, 3 dimensions with 10 values each could yield up to 1,000 series (10 × 10 × 10).
